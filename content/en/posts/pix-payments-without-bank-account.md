Pix is Brazil's dominant payment system. It is instant, works around the clock, and is accepted virtually everywhere — from street vendors to utility companies. But to send a Pix payment, you need a bank account or payment institution account in Brazil. If you are a foreigner living, working, or traveling in Brazil, opening a local bank account can be extremely difficult. The bureaucracy around documentation, CPF requirements, and proof of address makes it a non-starter for many.

The DePix App solves this. Using its withdrawal feature, you can send Pix payments to any Pix key in Brazil — even if you do not have a Brazilian bank account. All you need is DePix (a Brazilian real stablecoin) in a Liquid Network wallet. This guide walks you through the process step by step.

## How it works

Pix is an instant payment system operated by Brazil's Central Bank. Normally, sending a Pix payment requires a bank account connected to the system. The DePix App works as an intermediary: it receives your DePix tokens on the Liquid Network and, in return, sends a Pix payment to the recipient's bank account on your behalf.

Here is the flow: you hold DePix in your digital wallet. When you need to make a payment, you open the DePix App, enter the amount and the recipient's Pix key. The app generates a Liquid Network address for you to send your DePix to. Once the tokens arrive, the DePix App processes the payment and sends the Pix.

The recipient receives a standard Pix payment in their bank account. They do not need to know anything about cryptocurrency or the Liquid Network. From their side, it is a normal Pix transfer.

This means that if you hold DePix — regardless of how you obtained them — you can pay any person, business, or service that accepts Pix in Brazil, without needing a local bank account.

## Step by step: how to make a Pix payment with DePix

The process takes just a few minutes. Note: the DePix App interface is currently in Portuguese, so we include the original labels you will see on screen alongside their English translations.

**Step 1: Open the DePix App and select "Realizar pagamento" (Make a payment).** Go to [depixapp.com](https://depixapp.com/) in your browser. On the main screen, tap "Realizar pagamento" — the subtitle reads "Sacar DePix" (Withdraw DePix). This is the feature that converts your DePix into a Pix payment.

**Step 2: Enter the amount in the "Valor" (Amount) field.** Type in the value you want to send. The input accepts amounts in DePix (which is pegged 1:1 to the Brazilian real). You can toggle between two modes using the switch on screen:

- **"Valor que você envia"** (Amount you send) — the total that leaves your wallet, including fees.
- **"Valor que você recebe"** (Amount the recipient receives) — the net value that arrives at the destination, after fees.

This way you always know exactly how much will be debited and how much the recipient will get.

**Step 3: Enter the recipient's Pix key in the "Chave PIX" (Pix Key) field.** The placeholder reads "CPF, e-mail, celular ou chave aleatória" (CPF, email, phone, or random key). A Pix key can be any of these formats — enter whichever one the recipient has shared with you. CPF is a Brazilian tax ID number, but email and phone keys work just as well.

**Step 4: Tap "Solicitar transferência" (Request transfer).** After confirming the details, the DePix App displays a screen titled "Transferência criada!" (Transfer created!) with a Liquid Network address and a QR code.

**Step 5: Send your DePix to the generated address.** Open the SideSwap app (or any other Liquid wallet you use). You can either scan the QR code shown on the DePix App screen, or tap "Copiar endereço" (Copy address) and paste it into your wallet. Send exactly the amount of DePix indicated on screen — the app will display a warning reminding you to send the precise amount.

**Step 6: The DePix App processes and sends the Pix.** Once the DePix tokens are received on the Liquid Network, the app processes the transaction and sends the value via Pix to the specified key. The recipient receives the money in their bank account as a normal Pix payment.

The whole process takes just a few minutes from start to finish.

A common question: if the DePix App sends a Pix payment at the end, how is it different from a regular Pix?

With the DePix App, the payment originates from your DePix tokens, digital assets on the Liquid Network. You do not need a relationship with any Brazilian financial institution to send the payment.

For the recipient, nothing changes: they receive a standard Pix payment in their account. ## Security considerations

Before using the withdrawal feature, keep these security points in mind.

Always verify the Pix key before confirming. Once a Pix payment is sent, the transaction cannot be reversed. Make sure the key you entered is correct and corresponds to the intended recipient.

Keep your Liquid wallet secure. Since the DePix App is non-custodial, the security of your DePix depends on the security of your wallet. Back up your keys, use a strong password, and never share your credentials with anyone.

Review the amounts and fees before sending. The DePix App shows all necessary information on the confirmation screen. Read everything carefully before proceeding.
