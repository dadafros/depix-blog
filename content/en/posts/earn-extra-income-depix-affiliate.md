For context, the [DePix App](/en/posts/what-is-depix/) is a platform that lets users convert Brazilian reais into DePix, a stablecoin pegged to the real on the Liquid Network. Users deposit via Pix and receive DePix in their personal wallets, with privacy and self-custody. Every deposit made by someone you referred generates a commission for you.

The connection between you and your referrals has no expiration date. If someone signs up through your link today and makes a deposit two years from now, you still earn your commission on that deposit.

## How to get started: step by step

Getting set up as an affiliate takes just a few minutes. Here is the process:

**1. Create your DePix App account.** Go to [depixapp.com](https://depixapp.com/) and sign up. Registration requires only an email address — no documents, no identity verification, no lengthy onboarding process. Your account is active in seconds.

**2. Open the affiliate panel.** Inside the app, tap the menu icon (the ☰ in the corner of the screen). You will see the affiliate program option. Tap it to open your affiliate dashboard.

**3. Copy your referral link.** In the affiliate panel, you will find your unique referral link. This link is what identifies sign-ups as coming from you. Copy it with a single tap.

**4. Share the link.** Send it to friends, post it on social media, share it in messaging groups, add it to your social media bio — the format is entirely up to you. The more people who see your link, the more potential referrals you generate.

**5. Track your results.** The affiliate dashboard shows you in real time how many people have signed up through your link, the total deposit volume from your referrals, and your accumulated commission. Everything is transparent and easy to follow.

## How earnings work

The mechanics are straightforward: you earn a commission based on the deposit volume generated by the people you referred. The more your referrals use the DePix App, the more you earn.

Commissions are calculated on a monthly basis. At the end of each month, the DePix App tallies the total deposit volume from your referrals and calculates your commission accordingly. Payments are made directly to your account.

Key details about earnings:

- **There is no earnings cap.** The more people you refer and the more they use the platform, the higher your income.
- **Referrals are permanent.** A person who signed up through your link remains your referral forever. The connection does not expire after a certain period.
- **Tracking is fully transparent.** The dashboard displays all data in real time, so you always know exactly how much you have accumulated.
