You have DePix on the Liquid Network and want to convert them to reais in your bank account. The process is straightforward and fast. This guide walks you through every step of withdrawing DePix and receiving the funds via PIX, Brazil's instant payment system.

## Why withdraw DePix to reais?

Holding DePix on the Liquid Network offers genuine advantages: financial privacy, self-custody, and access to the Bitcoin ecosystem. But there are times when you need reais in your bank account — to pay bills, make purchases at shops that only accept traditional payment methods, or simply to have cash available.

The good news is that DePix was designed as a two-way bridge between crypto and fiat. Depositing is easy, and withdrawing is equally simple. If you are new to DePix, we recommend starting with our article on [what DePix is](/en/posts/what-is-depix/) to understand the basics.

## What you need to withdraw

To withdraw DePix and receive reais in your bank account, you need an active account on the [DePix App](https://depixapp.com/), DePix available at your registered Liquid Network address, and a valid PIX key (CPF, phone number, email, or random key) linked to your bank account.

The entire withdrawal process happens within the DePix App. You do not need any other platform, exchange, or external tool.

## Step-by-step withdrawal process

The withdrawal process in the DePix App is designed to be as direct as possible. Here is each step in detail.

First, open the [DePix App](https://depixapp.com/) and log into your account. On the main screen, you will see two options: deposit and withdrawal. Select the withdrawal option.

Next, enter the amount you want to withdraw in reais. The app will display the equivalent DePix amount that will be debited from your balance, including any applicable fees. Everything is shown transparently before you confirm.

Then, enter your PIX key. This can be your CPF, phone number, email, or a random key. Make sure the key is linked to the bank account where you want to receive the reais.

Review all the information — amount, fee, PIX key — and confirm the withdrawal. The app will process the operation.

Finally, wait for the PIX to arrive in your account. Since PIX is instant, the funds typically appear in your bank account within seconds. In rare cases, it may take a few minutes depending on the receiving bank.

That is the entire process. No paperwork, no multi-day waiting periods, no complicated forms.

## What does it cost?

DePix charges a fee for each withdrawal, which is displayed clearly on the confirmation screen before you authorize the operation. You will never be surprised by hidden charges.

Compared to other ways of converting cryptocurrency to fiat, DePix's cost is competitive. Traditional exchanges charge trading fees (typically 0.5% to 1%), plus withdrawal fees that can be fixed or percentage-based. With DePix, you pay a single transparent fee and that is it.

For larger amounts, the relative cost tends to be lower, making DePix especially advantageous for users moving more significant sums.

## How long does it take?

The total withdrawal time depends on two stages: processing by DePix and the PIX transfer through the banking system.

The DePix processing stage is fast. Once you confirm the withdrawal in the app, the operation is processed within minutes. The PIX stage is nearly instantaneous — in the vast majority of cases, the money appears in your account in under 30 seconds.

In total, from the moment you confirm the withdrawal to having the money available in your bank account, the entire process typically takes just a few minutes. Compare this to traditional exchanges that can take one to three business days to process a withdrawal.

## Withdrawal limits

The DePix App has withdrawal limits that are displayed within the app. These limits exist as a security measure and are adequate for most users.

If you need to withdraw larger amounts, the app will show the available limit for your profile. Unlike banks, which often reduce limits during nighttime hours or holidays, DePix limits are consistent regardless of the time of day.

## Tips for a smooth withdrawal

Double-check your PIX key before confirming. This seems obvious, but PIX key errors are the most common cause of withdrawal issues. Verify that the key is correct and linked to the right bank account.

If this is your first withdrawal, start with a small amount. Make a test withdrawal to familiarize yourself with the process and confirm everything works correctly with your PIX key. After that, you can withdraw larger amounts with full confidence.

Pay attention to your available balance. The amount you can withdraw depends on the DePix balance at your Liquid Network address. The app shows your updated balance on the main screen.

Keep a record. After each withdrawal, the app displays a confirmation. It is good practice to take a screenshot or note the transaction details for your personal records.

## What to do if the PIX does not arrive

In the vast majority of cases, the PIX arrives in seconds. But if for some reason the funds do not appear in your account, there are a few steps you can follow.

First, wait a few minutes. During peak hours or banking system maintenance windows, there may be a slight delay. The funds typically arrive within 30 minutes even in worst-case scenarios.

Second, verify that the PIX key you provided is correct and linked to the right account. A typo could cause the PIX transfer to be rejected.

If the funds still have not arrived, contact DePix support. The team can check the transaction status and help resolve any issues.
