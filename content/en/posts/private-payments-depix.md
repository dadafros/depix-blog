Think about what happens when you hand someone cash. The transaction is instant, direct, and private. No one in the middle monitors the amount, approves the transfer, or keeps a record. ## How a DePix payment works

The process is straightforward. Both the sender and the receiver need two things: a DePix App account and a Liquid wallet such as SideSwap.

When you want to send DePix to someone, here is the flow:

1. Open SideSwap on your phone.
2. Tap "Pay."
3. Paste the recipient's Liquid address.
4. Enter the amount of DePix you want to send.
5. Confirm the transaction.

In roughly two minutes, the DePix arrives in the recipient's wallet. No forms, no pending approvals, no business hours. The transaction travels directly from you to the recipient through the Liquid Network — a decentralized network that is not owned by any bank or corporation.

The cost? The Liquid network fee is practically zero — fractions of a cent. The DePix App charges absolutely nothing for wallet-to-wallet transfers. Compared to wire transfer fees or payment processor charges, the difference is dramatic.

## What makes these payments private

This is the fundamental difference between DePix and virtually every other digital payment method you have used.

The Liquid Network uses a technology called **Confidential Transactions**. By default, every transaction on the network hides the amount being transferred. External observers — anyone analyzing the blockchain — can see that a transaction occurred, but they cannot see how much was sent. Only the sender and the receiver know the value.

Compare this to the systems most people use today:

- **Traditional bank transfers**: the bank sees everything. Amount, date, time, sender, recipient. This information is available to the financial institution and, by extension, to any authority that requests it.
- **Bitcoin**: the blockchain is public. Anyone can see the amount of any transaction by checking a block explorer.
- **DePix on the Liquid Network**: the amount is hidden by default. The transaction is visible on the chain, but the value is cryptographically shielded.

This is not an optional feature you need to activate. Confidential Transactions are the default behavior of the Liquid Network. Every DePix transaction is born private.

For anyone who values [financial privacy](/en/posts/financial-privacy-depix/), this changes the equation entirely. Your transactions stop being an open book for banks, data analytics firms, and any other curious intermediary.

## What makes these payments permissionless

The word "permissionless" is central to understanding the power of DePix. In practical terms, it means no one needs to authorize your transactions.

Consider how a traditional bank transfer works. The bank reviews the operation, may impose daily limits, may hold funds for "security analysis," and in extreme cases can freeze your entire account. You depend on an intermediary's permission to move your own money.

With DePix on the Liquid Network, that dynamic does not exist:

- **No bank approval required**: there is no bank involved in the transaction. No one needs to say "yes" for your payment to go through.
- **No third-party limits**: the Liquid Network does not impose value caps. You decide how much to send.
- **No account freezes**: no one can freeze the DePix in your wallet. You have full custody of your funds.
- **Available 24/7/365**: there is no "banking hours" concept on the Liquid Network. Weekends, holidays, the middle of the night — the network is always running.
- **No reversals**: once confirmed, the transaction is final. No one can reverse, cancel, or charge back the payment without the recipient's consent.

This is the essence of being a [sovereign individual](/en/posts/sovereign-individual-permissionless-money/) over your own money. You do not ask permission to spend, send, or receive. You simply do it.

In every one of these scenarios, the common thread is the same: you transfer value directly to another person, without intermediaries that monitor, delay, or block the operation.

## Step-by-step guide: how to send DePix to someone

If you have never made a DePix payment before, follow this complete walkthrough:

Go to [depixapp.com](https://depixapp.com/) and sign up. The process is quick and straightforward.

**2. Set up your Liquid wallet**
Download SideSwap on your phone and configure your wallet. This is where your DePix will be stored — under your full control.

**3. Deposit Brazilian reais via Pix**
In the DePix App, make a deposit using Pix. Your reais are converted to DePix at a 1:1 ratio and sent to your Liquid wallet.

**4. Get the recipient's Liquid address**
The recipient opens SideSwap and taps "Receive" to generate a Liquid address. They share that address with you — via message, QR code, or any other method.

**5. Send the payment**
Open SideSwap, tap "Pay," paste the Liquid address, enter the amount, and confirm. The transaction is broadcast to the network immediately.

**6. Wait for confirmation**
In approximately two minutes, the DePix appears in the recipient's wallet. Transaction complete.

**7. The recipient decides what to do next**
The person who received the DePix can hold it in their wallet, swap it for Bitcoin or USDt within SideSwap, or withdraw to a bank account via Pix through the DePix App.

That is it. Six steps to send money with privacy, without a bank, and without asking anyone for permission.
