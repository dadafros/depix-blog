## Who we are

The DePix App Blog is maintained by the team at [DePix App](https://depixapp.com/), the first Brazilian stablecoin on the Liquid Network. Our goal is to educate readers about financial privacy, digital sovereignty, and the practical use of cryptocurrency in everyday life.

## Blog mission

We believe that clear, accessible information is the first step toward financial sovereignty. This blog exists to:

- **Educate** about financial privacy, cryptocurrency, and the DePix ecosystem
- **Guide** users with practical tutorials and step-by-step instructions
- **Inform** about regulation, taxes, and security in the use of digital assets

## Editorial commitment

All content published on this blog follows strict editorial principles:

- **Accuracy**: Information about fees, limits, processing times, and features is verified directly with the DePix App product team before publication.
- **Official sources**: When covering regulatory or tax topics, we cite official sources such as Brazil's Federal Revenue Service (Receita Federal), the Central Bank of Brazil, and applicable legislation.
- **Updates**: Posts are reviewed periodically to reflect changes in the product, regulations, or market conditions. The last update date is displayed when applicable.
- **Honesty**: We do not make promises of financial gains. We present the trade-offs of each tool — Bitcoin for store of value, DePix for private payments, Pix for convenience.

## About DePix

DePix is a digital Brazilian real (1 DePix = 1 BRL) on the Liquid Network, the Bitcoin sidechain developed by Blockstream. The DePix App connects Pix to the Liquid Network, enabling private payments and self-custody without the need for documents or a bank account.

- **Website**: [depixapp.com](https://depixapp.com/)
- **Support**: available directly in the app

## Important notice

The content on this blog is informational and educational. It does not constitute financial, tax, or legal advice. Consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.
